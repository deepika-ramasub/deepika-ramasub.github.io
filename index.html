About Me

Hi there, I'm Deepika! I am a PhD student at the University of Colorado Boulder in Information Science advised by Dr. Leysia Palen.
I earned my Masters of Science in Computer Science and Applications at Virginia Tech in 2020 advised by Dr. Tanushree Mitra. I received my Bachelors in Technology in Computer Science and Engineering at the National Institute of Technology, Tiruchirappalli.

My work is predominantly interested in mis- and disinformation around female reproductive health conditions. Previously, our group at PalenLab and I worked on researching online misinformation/disinformation around the COVID vaccine as it is targeted towards women and other marginalized communities to deliberately suppress vaccine compliance.
