About Me

Hi there! I'm Deepika, a PhD student at the University of Colorado Boulder in Information Science advised by Dr. Leysia Palen.
I earned my Masters of Science in Computer Science and Applications at Virginia Tech in 2020 advised by Dr. Tanushree Mitra. I received my Bachelors in Technology in Computer Science and Engineering at the National Institute of Technology, Tiruchirappalli.

My work is predominantly situated in the intersection of marginalized communities + technology. At the moment, I am working to understand the data infrastructures and practices of care groups that are supporting forced migrants affected by the U.S. Immigration Crisis.
In a second project, our group at PalenLab and I are working on research that examines online misinformation/disinformation around the COVID vaccine as it is targeted towards women and other marginalized communities to deliberately suppress vaccine compliance.
